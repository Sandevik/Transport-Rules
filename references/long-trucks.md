## Overview

Sweden permits two categories of long combination vehicles (LCV) beyond the standard EU 18.75 m road train:

| Category | Max length | Status |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| European Modular System (EMS) | **25.25 m** | Permitted on most main roads |
| Long Combination Vehicle (LCV) | **34.5 m** | Permitted on approved network only |

The 34.5 m vehicles are a specifically Swedish (and Nordic) innovation driven by the forest and bulk transport industries. They were introduced on an expanded approved road network in December 2023.

---

## 25.25 m — European Modular System (EMS)

### Rules
- Permitted throughout Sweden on roads capable of handling the combination
- Must be capable of completing a turn within a **25.0 m turning circle** (swept path)
- Maximum gross weight: **74 tonnes** (on BK1 or BK4 roads)
- Standard driving licence for the vehicle category — no special permit for the combination
- Driver training required on EMS-specific handling

### Typical EMS configurations
- Dragbil (tractor unit) + 2 påhängsvagnar (semi-trailers)
- Lastbil + dolly + påhängsvagn
- Lastbil + länkesläp

## 34.5 m — Long Combination Vehicles

### Legal basis
**Trafikförordningen (SFS 1998:1276), 4 kap. 17f §** — authorises operation of vehicle combinations up to 34.5 m on designated roads.

**TSFS 2025:17** — Trafikverkets föreskrifter om långa fordonskombinationer — the detailed regulation governing 34.5 m vehicles (entry into force 2025, replacing earlier provisional rules).

### Approved road network
- **Approximately 590 km** of roads opened for 34.5 m vehicles as of December 2023
- Network concentrated on:
  - Major timber transport corridors (Norrland, Svealand)
  - Industrial ports and logistics hubs
  - Specific motorway sections
- Trafikverket publishes the approved network map:
  https://bransch.trafikverket.se/for-dig-i-branschen/vag/langa-lastbilar/tillstandet-natet/
- **Do not operate a 34.5 m combination outside the approved network**

### Maximum gross weight
- On BK1/BK4 roads: **74 tonnes** (same as EMS)
- Axle load limits: as per BK class (see `bk-bearing-classes.md`)

### Speed limits
- Maximum **80 km/h** on all roads for 34.5 m combinations
- Even where the road has a higher posted limit, the 34.5 m combination is limited to 80 km/h

### Vehicle combinations permitted at 34.5 m
Per TSFS 2025:17, permitted combinations include:

| Type | Description |
|------|------------|
| A-train | Lastbil + dolly + påhängsvagn |
| B-train | Dragbil + B-kopplingspåhängsvagn + påhängsvagn |
| C-train | Lastbil + länkesläp (central axle trailer) |
| Modular combinations | Various approved multi-unit configurations |

Specific vehicle type approval requirements apply to each combination. Vehicles must be type-approved for 34.5 m operation.

### Driver requirements
- Valid driving licence for the vehicle category (CE or DE)
- Yrkesförarkompetens (CPC) — professional driver competence card
- Specific training on 34.5 m vehicle handling (company responsibility)
- No separate dispensation permit required — operating on approved network is sufficient

### Equipment requirements (TSFS 2025:17)
- Electronic stability control (ESP/EBS) on all axles
- Air suspension or equivalent on all driven/load-bearing axles
- Rear marking lights and conspicuity markings per regulations
- The combination must meet turning circle requirements (swept path within approved limits)
- Coupling and drawbar equipment must be type-approved for the combination

## 34.5 m vs Transportdispens

| Aspect | 34.5 m on approved network | Transportdispens (>25.25 m) |
|--------|--------------------------|----------------------------|
| Permit needed | No (network approval sufficient) | Yes, per transport |
| Network restriction | Approved 590 km network | Route-specific approval |
| Lead time | None | Days to months |
| Pilot vehicle | Generally not required | May be required |
| Speed | 80 km/h max | As specified in permit |

If a 34.5 m route includes roads NOT on the approved network, a separate transportdispens is required for those road segments.

---

## Sanctions for Non-Compliance

Operating a 34.5 m vehicle outside the approved network or in violation of TSFS 2025:17 can result in:
- **Överlastavgift** if weight limits are exceeded (SFS 1972:435)
- Administrative sanctions from Transportstyrelsen
- Forced stop of the vehicle
