## Overview

Anyone conducting **yrkesmässig trafik** (professional transport — i.e., transport for payment or compensation) must hold a **trafiktillstånd** (traffic permit / commercial traffic license) issued by Transportstyrelsen.

Two main types:
- **Gods- och bussrafiktillstånd** — goods and bus transport (EU Regulation 1071/2009)
- **Taxitrafiktillstånd** — taxi transport (Swedish Taxi Traffic Act, see taxi.md)

---

## The Four Requirements (EU 1071/2009, Article 3)

All applicants must satisfy **all four** conditions:

### 1. Establishment in Sweden (Etablering)
The company must be genuinely and stably established in Sweden:
- Must have premises where it keeps core business documents
- Must have vehicles registered or authorized in Sweden
- Must effectively and continuously manage transport operations from Swedish premises

### 2. Good Repute (Gott anseende)
The company, its directors, and the traffic manager must not have any disqualifying convictions:
- No serious criminal convictions (fraud, trafficking, drug offences, etc.)
- No serious road traffic violations (repeated speeding, DUI, etc.)
- No serious violations of road transport regulations (working time, weights, cabotage, etc.)
- Tax compliance (inga allvarliga skatteskulder)
- Good repute is assessed on a continuous basis — it can be revoked

### 3. Financial Standing (Ekonomisk förmåga)
The company must demonstrate sufficient financial capacity:

| Vehicle number | Required capital |
|---------------|-----------------|
| First vehicle | **9,000 EUR** |
| Each additional vehicle | **5,000 EUR** |

Financial capacity may be demonstrated by:
- Bank guarantee
- Insurance policy
- Certified annual accounts showing net assets

*Note: These are EU minimum amounts. Sweden applies them directly in EUR or the SEK equivalent.*

### 4. Professional Competence (Yrkeskunnande)
The company must have at least one **traffic manager** (trafikansvarig) who:
- Has passed the approved CPC knowledge examination (for transport management — different from driver CPC)
- Is genuinely responsible for the transport operations
- Has a real link to the undertaking (employed, director, owner, or authorized representative)

The traffic manager examination covers: civil and commercial law, social law, fiscal law, business and financial management, access to the market, technical standards, road safety.

## Types of Traffic Permit

| Permit type | Scope |
|------------|-------|
| Nationellt godstrafiktillstånd | Domestic goods transport in Sweden |
| Gemenskapstillstånd (Community license) | International goods transport within EU/EEA |
| Persontrafiktillstånd | Passenger transport (buses) |
| Taxitrafiktillstånd | Taxi operations |

A Community license (gemenskapstillstånd) is required for all international professional goods transport within the EU/EEA. Each vehicle must carry a **certified true copy**.

---

## Application Process

1. Apply to **Transportstyrelsen** (online or paper form)
2. Submit documentation of all four requirements
3. Transportstyrelsen reviews and may request additional information
4. Permit issued if all conditions met
5. Permit must be renewed / reviewed periodically

---

## Ongoing Obligations

After receiving a permit, the holder must:
- Continuously satisfy all four requirements
- Notify Transportstyrelsen of changes (traffic manager changes, financial difficulties, serious violations)
- Annual reporting of employee numbers (deadline: **31 March** each year)
- Ensure drivers hold valid YKB (see professional-driver.md)
- Comply with all kör- och vilotidsregler

---

## Revocation and Sanctions

Transportstyrelsen may **revoke** (återkalla) a traffic permit if:
- Good repute is lost (serious convictions, repeated violations)
- Financial capacity falls below minimum
- Establishment requirement no longer met
- Traffic manager no longer qualifies

**Serious violations** that can trigger loss of good repute include:
- Systematic violations of driving/rest time rules
- Cabotage violations
- Undeclared labour / illegal employment
- Tax fraud

**Operating without a required permit:**
- Domestic: fines or **imprisonment up to 1 year**
- International: fines only

## Beställaransvar (Orderer Responsibility)

Anyone ordering transport must verify the carrier holds a valid permit. See `company-control.md` for details.
