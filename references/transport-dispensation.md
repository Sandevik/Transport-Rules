## What is a Transportdispens?

A **transportdispens** (transport dispensation/permit) is required when a vehicle or load exceeds the standard limits for dimensions (length, width, height) or weight set in Trafikförordningen. The permit specifies:
- Approved route
- Time of travel (time windows, day restrictions)
- Speed limits
- Requirements for pilot vehicles (vägtransportledare)
- Any other conditions

**Legal basis:** Trafikförordningen (SFS 1998:1276), **13 kap. 3 §** — Trafikverket or the road authority grants dispensation for transport exceeding standard limits.

---

## Permit Categories (L, XL, XXL)

Trafikverket classifies transport dispensation applications into three categories based on the size and weight of the load:

### L — Large (Stor)
- Exceeds standard limits but within moderate oversized range
- Simpler application process
- Usually approved within **days to a few weeks**
- May require pilot vehicle (if width > 3.0 m or as specified)

### XL — Extra Large (Extra Stor)
- Significant exceedance of standard limits
- Requires route survey and bridge analysis
- Approval time: typically **weeks to months**
- Pilot/escort vehicles required
- Police notification required

### XXL — Very Extra Large (Mycket Extra Stor)
- Extreme loads (typically **>150 tonnes** or very large dimensions)
- Full route engineering survey required
- Bridge and road structure analysis by Trafikverket
- **Lead time: minimum 12 months** before transport
- Multiple escort vehicles, police escort mandatory
- Trafikverket may require temporary road reinforcement
- International coordination if crossing borders

## The Trix System

**Trix** is Trafikverket's digital system for applying for and managing transport dispensation permits.

- **Web portal:** https://bransch.trafikverket.se/for-dig-i-branschen/vag/transportdispens/trix/
- Applications are submitted electronically via Trix
- Status tracking and permit download through Trix
- Required for all new dispensation applications

### What you need for a Trix application:
1. Company registration number (organisationsnummer)
2. Vehicle registration number(s)
3. Load dimensions: length, width, height, weight
4. Proposed route (start point, end point, specific road numbers preferred)
5. Proposed transport dates/time window
6. Contact information for transport coordinator

---

## Required Documentation

### All categories (L, XL, XXL):
- Vehicle registration certificate (registreringsbevis)
- Load description with dimensions and weight distribution
- Drawing/diagram showing load on vehicle
- Route plan

### XL and XXL additionally:
- Axle load diagram (axellastvärden)
- Bridge calculation if route includes older bridges
- Pilot vehicle arrangement plan
- Police permit application (Polismyndighetens tillstånd)

### XXL additionally:
- Structural engineering report (Trafikverket-commissioned)
- Emergency plan
- Insurance certificate for abnormal load

## Standard Limits Requiring Dispensation

Transport exceeding these limits requires a permit:

| Dimension | Standard limit | Dispensation needed above |
|-----------|--------------|--------------------------|
| Width | 2.55 m (2.60 m refrigerated) | > 2.55 m / 2.60 m |
| Height | 4.5 m | > 4.5 m |
| Length (single vehicle) | 12.0 m | > 12.0 m |
| Length (road train) | 25.25 m | > 25.25 m |
| Gross weight | Per BK class (up to 74 t BK1/BK4) | Exceeding BK class limit |

---

## Pilot Vehicle Requirements (Vägtransportledare)

A certified **vägtransportledare** (road transport leader) is required as escort when:
- Width exceeds **3.0 m** (front escort)
- Width exceeds **3.5 m** (front + rear escort)
- Length exceeds **30 m** (rear escort)
- Load is particularly hazardous or visibility is impaired

See `road-transport-leader.md` for certification requirements.

---

## Speed Limits for Oversized Transport

| Width | Max speed |
|-------|----------|
| 2.55 – 3.0 m | Road's normal speed limit |
| 3.0 – 3.5 m | 70 km/h |
| > 3.5 m | 50 km/h (or as specified in permit) |

Night transport and weekend restrictions may apply depending on width and route.

---

## Time Restrictions

- Transport > 3.0 m wide: **prohibited on weekends and public holidays** unless specifically permitted
- Transport > 4.0 m wide: **only during daylight hours** (varies by season — check permit)
- Christmas, Easter, Midsummer periods: stricter restrictions apply

---

## Fees

Application fees are charged by Trafikverket. Fee levels depend on category:
- L category: moderate administrative fee
- XL category: higher fee, includes route assessment
- XXL category: significant fee, includes engineering assessment
