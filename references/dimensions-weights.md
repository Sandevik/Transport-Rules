## Length Limits (Längdbestämmelser)

Sweden has three tiers of maximum vehicle length:

| Category | Max Length |
|----------|-----------|
| Standard vehicle combinations | **24.0 metres** |
| European Modular System (EMS) | **25.25 metres** |
| Long Combination Vehicles (LCV) | **34.5 metres** (special permit required) |

### Standard Rules (≤24.0 m)
- Single truck: **12.0 m**
- Articulated truck (dragbil + påhängsvagn): **16.5 m**
- Road train (lastbil + släpvagn): **18.75 m**
- Bus (rigid): **15.0 m**
- Articulated bus: **18.75 m**

### European Modular System — 25.25 m
Allows vehicle combinations up to 25.25 m. Specific route and vehicle requirements apply. These combinations must be capable of turning in a 25 m radius sweep.

### Long Combination Vehicles — up to 34.5 m
Requires special dispensation (dispenstillstånd). Used primarily for timber transport and specific industrial transport routes. Must follow approved routes.

---

## Width Limits (Breddbestämmelser)

| Vehicle type | Max Width |
|-------------|-----------|
| Standard motor vehicles | **2.55 m** |
| Refrigerated/insulated vehicles | **2.60 m** |
| Agricultural/forestry equipment | **3.00 m** (with conditions) |

Loads wider than the vehicle: special transport rules apply (bred last / dispenstransport).

---

## Height Limits (Höjdbestämmelser)

- Standard maximum height: **4.5 metres** on Swedish roads
- Some roads and bridges have lower restrictions — always check route before transport
- Oversized height requires special transport planning (vägtransportledare)

## Weight Limits (Viktbestämmelser)

### Axle Loads (Axeltryck)

| Axle type | Standard roads (BK1) | Reduced capacity roads (BK2) | BK3 roads | BK4 roads |
|-----------|---------------------|----------------------------|-----------|-----------|
| Single driving axle | 11.5 t | 10 t | 8 t | 11.5 t |
| Single non-driving axle | 10 t | 8 t | 8 t | 10 t |

*BK = Bärighetsklass (load-bearing class). Most main roads are BK1.*

### Bogie Load (Boggitryck — 2 axles)

| Axle spacing | BK1 | BK2 | BK3 |
|-------------|-----|-----|-----|
| < 1.0 m | 11 t | 10 t | 8 t |
| 1.0–1.3 m | 16 t | 13 t | 11 t |
| > 1.3 m | 18 t | 15 t | 11 t |

### Triple Axle Load (Trippelaxeltryck — 3 axles)

| Axle spacing | BK1 | BK2 | BK3 |
|-------------|-----|-----|-----|
| ≤ 1.3 m | 21 t | 18 t | 11 t |
| > 1.3 m | 24 t | 20 t | 11 t |

### Total Weight (Totalvikt / Bruttovikt)

| Vehicle type | Max weight (BK1) |
|-------------|-----------------|
| 2-axle truck | 18 t |
| 3-axle truck | 25 t |
| 4-axle truck | 31 t (32 t with air suspension) |
| 4-axle articulated (standard) | 36 t |
| 5-axle articulated | 40 t |
| 6-axle articulated | 42 t |
| Road train (lastbil + släp), up to 7 axles | Up to **64 t** (BK1, with conditions) |
| EMS combinations (25.25 m) | Up to **74 t** |

**Note:** Sweden permits significantly higher maximum weights than the EU minimum (40 t) due to the forest industry and road network design. The 64–74t limits apply to specific vehicle configurations and road classes.

### Winter Restrictions (Tjällossning)

During spring thaw ("tjällossning"), typically **10 November – 10 April**, some roads switch to BK2 or BK3 restrictions. Check the Swedish Transport Administration (Trafikverket) for current restrictions.

---

## Oversized / Overweight Transport (Dispenstransport)

Transport exceeding standard limits requires:
1. **Special permit (dispensansökan)** from Trafikverket or the road authority
2. **Vägtransportledare** (road transport leader) escort for very large loads
3. Route planning and sometimes police escort

See `road-transport-leader.md` for escort requirements.

---

## Overload Fee (Överlastavgift)

When actual weight exceeds permitted maximum, an **överlastavgift** is imposed on the vehicle owner.

Fee is calculated as a proportion of the overload amount. See `sanctions-penalties.md` for details.

**Legal basis:** SFS 1972:435 — Lag om överlastavgift
