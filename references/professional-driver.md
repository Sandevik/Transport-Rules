---

## What is Yrkesförarkompetens?

Yrkesförarkompetens (YKB) is the Swedish implementation of the EU Certificate of Professional Competence (CPC) for professional drivers. It is required for commercial driving of heavy vehicles.

The system has existed since:
- **10 September 2008** — passenger transport (buses)
- **10 September 2009** — goods transport (trucks)

---

## Who Needs It?

YKB is required for **all professional drivers** operating vehicles requiring the following driving licenses:

| License category | Vehicle type |
|----------------|-------------|
| C1, C1E | Light trucks 3.5–7.5t |
| C, CE | Heavy trucks >7.5t |
| D1, D1E | Minibuses |
| D, DE | Buses |

**Applies across all 29 EEA states** (all EU members + Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein).

**Does NOT apply to:**
- Drivers who obtained their license before the cutoff dates AND held it continuously (acquired rights — grandfathering)
- Drivers of vehicles exempt from EU 561/2006 (see exceptions.md)

---

## Initial Qualification (Grundutbildning)

New professional drivers must complete mandatory initial training and pass a knowledge test.

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Training | Mandatory basic training at approved provider |
| Knowledge test | Must be passed to receive the certificate |
| Certificate validity | **5 years** |
| Age exception | Young drivers can begin CPC training earlier than standard minimum age for that license category |

**Training providers:** Authorized training schools, secondary schools (gymnasieskola) with approved transport programs, and adult education programs (vuxenutbildning).

---

## Periodic Training (Fortbildning)

After the initial 5-year certificate expires, drivers must complete **periodic training** to renew:

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| Total training hours | **35 hours** per 5-year cycle |
| Minimum module size | **7 hours** per module |
| Number of modules | Typically 5 × 7 hours |
| Distance learning cap | Maximum **1 module (7 hours)** may be distance-based |
| Overall distance cap | Maximum **12 hours** distance learning per certification period |
| Combined category | If driver holds both goods AND passenger transport CPC, **one 35-hour program** satisfies both |

Training must be completed at approved (Transportstyrelsen-recognized) training centers.

---

## The CPC Card / Proof of Competence

**Bevis om yrkesförarkompetens (YKB-kort):**
- Demonstrated by entry on the **driving license** (code 95 in field 12) OR
- A separate **Driver Qualification Card (DKC)**
- The card/license entry shows the expiry date of the current certification period

**From 1 January 2025:** Transportstyrelsen re-introduced **fees** for issuing YKB certificates. Invoices are sent to applicants after the certificate is issued.

**Subpage:** https://www.transportstyrelsen.se/sv/vagtrafik/yrkestrafik/gods-och-buss/yrkesforarkompetens/

---

## Mutual Recognition

CPC qualifications are recognized across all 29 EEA states. A driver with a valid Finnish CPC may drive commercially in Sweden without re-qualifying.

---

## Penalties for Non-Compliance

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|------------|
| Driving commercially without valid YKB | Sanction fee / fine |
| Company using drivers without YKB | Sanction fee imposed on the company |
| Expired YKB — continuing to drive commercially | Same as above |

---

## Administrative Notes

- Applications and certificate issuance handled by **Transportstyrelsen**
- Training courses must be registered with Transportstyrelsen
- Employers must verify that their drivers' YKB is current and valid
- Training records are maintained in the national register
